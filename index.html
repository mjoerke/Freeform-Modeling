This website presents the Freeform Modeling Workshop, lead by Volkmar Meyer Schönbohm at the HfG Schwäbisch Gmünd.  The workshop includes materials and tools for manipulating clay and gypsum plaster. We conclude with a reflection on how freeform modelling can be a meditative and transformative experience.
